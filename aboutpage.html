Megzit provides reliable fiber internet to various areas in Gilgil, including Gilgil Town, Teachers A & B, Langalanga, Ridgemount, Barnabas, and more. We are continuously expanding our service coverage to bring fast internet to more communities.

Our installation process is quick and hassle-free. After signing up, our professional technicians will schedule an appointment and install the fiber connection at your location. Installation typically takes a few hours, and you can start enjoying high-speed internet right away.

The standard installation fee for Megzit fiber internet is KES 4,500. This one-time fee covers all setup costs, and our technicians will ensure a smooth and efficient installation experience.

Megzit offers a variety of affordable internet packages to suit different needs, including 8mbps, 15mbps, and 25mbps plans.
